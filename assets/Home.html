Empower Through Books uses the power of business to change the world by offering quality books. We collect and sell books online to donate books and fund literacy initiatives worldwide.We were found in 2016 by two friends from the Northwest Missouri State University. We are not a traditional company with an addon cause component.Social and environmental responsibity is at the core of our business.






Who we are?

Empower Through Books is a locally owned independent bookshop committed to offering a quality selection of affordable newbooks, bargain and electronic books, as well as gifts and educational items for customers of all ages and backgrounds. We are committed to engage with the diverse peoples and organizations of our community, particularly in the Northwest Missouri State University, but also on a national and international scale, to work towards the goals of a knowledgeable public and environmentally sustainable planet.

I am assionate about the written word and seek to share my love of reading to help create and sustain a community of lifelong readers.

Thanks for joining us in our mission to create a better world.
